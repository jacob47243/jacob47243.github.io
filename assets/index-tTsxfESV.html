Approach

  • Sensor calibration and normalization by dynamic pressure to stabilize across regimes.
  • Feature engineering → trained regressors for α, β with cross-validation.
  • Dense α/β grid evaluation to catch bias bands and drift; iterative tuning.

Results & Takeaways

  • Representative validation errors ~MSE ≈ 0.8–2.0 deg² depending on operating point (see plots).
  • Calibrated pipeline (ingest → calibrate → train → validate → export) is ready for onboard inference.
  • Next: fuse IMU/GPS, extend to full flight envelope, and harden for real-time deployment.
